Chocolate Chip Banana Bread Recipe Ingredients For the batter: 2 ripe bananas ½ cup melted butter ¾ cup brown sugar 2 eggs 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon baking soda ½ teaspoon salt ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon ¾ cup chocolate chips For the topping: 2 tablespoons chocolate chips 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts Instructions Mash the bananas in a large bowl until smooth. Add the melted butter, brown sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract. Mix until combined. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon. Add the dry ingredients into the wet mixture and stir until a batter forms. Fold in the chocolate chips. Pour the batter into a greased loaf tin and sprinkle the topping ingredients over the top. Pre-heat the oven to 350F or 180C. Bake the banana bread for 45-50 minutes until golden brown and a toothpick comes out clean. Allow the bread to cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
